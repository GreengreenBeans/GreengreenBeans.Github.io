The GreenBeans Team

We are a small team of five international students who met in Jersey during the first JCG LEAP leadership program. Our aim is to share our passion to help the bee population thrive throughout the world. 

The protection of bees is vital for the environment, 85% of crops depend on bees. These crops feed our cattle which in turn provide us with sources of meat and dairy products, therefore, protecting the bees has an impact on our survival.
